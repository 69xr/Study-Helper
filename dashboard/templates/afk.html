ℹ️ How the AFK System Works
💬
/afk [reason]
Member runs this command. Bot renames them to [AFK] Name and stores their reason.
🔔
Mention Detection
When someone pings an AFK user, the bot replies with an embed showing the reason and how long they've been away.
👋
Auto-Return
When the AFK member sends any message, the bot removes their AFK, restores their nickname, and shows a welcome-back embed.
